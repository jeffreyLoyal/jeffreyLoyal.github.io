🌐 Personal Website: JeffreyHoyle.com

🔗 Link: jeffreyhoyle.com

In my **sophomore year of high school**, I built JeffreyHoyle.com, a website dedicated to my goalkeeping highlights as I was actively being recruited for college soccer. This project served as my introduction to web design and personal branding.

Through this experience, I:

  • Learned how to design and structure websites using WordPress as my main platform.
  • Gained hands-on experience in content management, SEO, and digital marketing to grow engagement.
  • Developed a passion for business, marketing, and web development, which influenced my career direction.

This project not only showcased my athletic career but also gave me technical skills in web development and online engagement.

📊 Marketing Analytics & Data Storytelling (San Jose Sharks Case Study)

📂 File: Download Marketing Data & Analytics Report (PDF)

As part of my Marketing Analytics class at DU, I conducted a data-driven analysis on the San Jose Sharks, focusing on strategies to increase fan engagement within the organization.

This project involved:

  • 📊 Data Collection: Sourced and compiled industry data on sports engagement and fan behavior.
  • 📈 Tableau Visualization: Used Tableau to visualize key metrics, compare historical trends, and identify opportunities.
  • 📝 Data Storytelling: Translated raw data into meaningful insights to present a compelling business case.

Outcome: I developed an actionable strategy for improving fan interaction, digital marketing efforts, and in-game engagement, which could be implemented by the organization to drive attendance and loyalty.
